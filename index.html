Welcome to Sandy's Tower Hotel, a beacon of luxury and elegance in the heart of Bhubaneswar, Odisha. Established in 2012, our prestigious 4-star hotel has become a preferred destination for travelers seeking comfort and sophistication

Located in Bhubaneswar, the City of Temples, Sandy's Tower Hotel offers a serene and luxurious stay with world-class amenities. We offer four room categories to select from, ensuring a perfect fit for every guest. Our hotel features a state-of-the-art fitness center, a refreshing swimming pool, multiple dining options, and a stylish bar, WiFi, 3 banquet halls and 2 outdoor lawns. At Sandy's Tower Hotel, we pride ourselves on exceptional service.
